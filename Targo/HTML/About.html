About Us

As the Targo team, we recognize the crucial importance of agriculture. With the ever-increasing global population and rising consumption demands, the significance of agricultural production is growing every day. Aware of this reality, we embarked on a journey to integrate agriculture with technology, aiming to safeguard our future. Our goal is to illuminate the future with innovative projects that ensure sustainable and efficient production models.
In today’s dynamic and rapidly changing world, leveraging the capabilities of technology can make agricultural production more effective and efficient. The application of advanced technologies not only enhances productivity in agricultural activities but also supports environmental sustainability. At Targo, we are committed to combining these two critical elements to revolutionize agriculture.
Our projects aim to utilize cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things (IoT), and data analytics at every stage of agriculture. By doing so, we plan to increase productivity in agricultural production, meeting the needs of both producers and consumers.
The future of agriculture will be shaped by the power of technology, and we aspire to be at the forefront of this transformation. Through our ongoing and upcoming projects, we aim to shed light on the agricultural sector and build a sustainable future. At Targo, we are determined to continue creating future-oriented solutions by integrating agriculture with technology.
